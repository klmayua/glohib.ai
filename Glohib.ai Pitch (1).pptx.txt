The global health internship ecosystem suffers from significant inefficiencies. Global health professionals face limited access to verified, relevant, and personalized opportunities, often encountering static or keyword-based platforms. Concurrently, organizations struggle with lengthy recruitment cycles, unreliable applicant filtering, and a lack of structured, scalable assessment for intern readiness.

Intelligent Discovery & Feed Generation: Traditional platforms cannot provide personalized, verified internship feeds. The technical hurdle is moving beyond static listings to a dynamic system that intelligently matches opportunities to individual student profiles based on nuanced criteria. 
AI-Powered Application Scoring Engine: Manual or basic filtering of applications is time-consuming and prone to human bias, leading to unreliable applicant quality assessment. The technical challenge is building an automated, objective, and scalable engine for initial application evaluation. 
Adaptive Multi-Stage Screening Pipeline: Beyond initial applications, there's a critical gap in structured, scalable assessment of intern readiness. The technical complexity lies in generating unique, adaptive assessment questions and accurately grading diverse response types (text, video) at scale. 
Mentorship Routing System: A significant technical challenge is creating a robust system to intelligently match non-qualifying candidates with relevant mentors based on specialization, availability, and user goals, as well as to manage the booking and interaction process.
Employer Dashboard & Final Handoff: Employers are overwhelmed by large volumes of unqualified applicants. The technical hurdle is securely integrating a highly filtered, AI-vetted candidate pipeline with an intuitive employer dashboard that provides detailed, actionable insights into each candidate's performance.

The GLOHIB MVP is laser-focused on delivering a fully functional internship discovery, assessment, and mentorship flow. The following features are live or in final development for this MVP: 
Our MVP’s Core Functionality
Situational Analysis (Pass Only): Dynamically generated questions using prompt-tuned AI models, graded with an NLP-based evaluation engine. 
Mentorship Booking (Fair Candidates): Fair candidates can select a mentor by area of specialization, book a session, and receive guidance. 
CV Revamp Guidance (Fail Candidates): Fail candidates are routed to a CV enhancement interface with AI tips and suggestions. 
Video Interview Assessment (Final Stage): Unique, timed video questions, with AI grading of speech clarity, content quality, and presence metrics. 
Employer Dashboard (Read-Only Mode): Verified employers see fully evaluated applicants with breakdowns (CV, scores, video, etc.) for decision-making.
